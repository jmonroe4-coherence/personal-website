A Space for Integration

I am a Master's student in Clinical Mental Health Counseling at Merrimack College. My clinical practice is founded on a deep respect for the individual and the belief that you already possess the innate drive toward wholeness.

Therapy with me involves looking at how your brain and body work together to navigate the world. I integrate the Theory of Constructed Emotion with Coherence Therapy to help you update outdated patterns and find a new sense of freedom in your daily life.

How We Work Together

Healing happens when we address both the story in your mind and the sensations in your body

Mind & Science

We use the Theory of Constructed Emotion to understand how your brain creates your reality. This helps us approach your symptoms with curiosity and logic rather than shame.

Body & Sensation

Earthy somatic work helps you reconnect with your physical self. We focus on interoception to help your nervous system feel safe and grounded in the present moment.

Root Transformation

Through memory reconsolidation and Coherence Therapy, we aim for true transformation. We look beyond surface relief to update the emotional roots of your experiences.
